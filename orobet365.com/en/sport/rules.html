  • Types of bets:

    On the site you will find the following types of bets:

    1. «Single bet» – bet, predicting the outcome of one event. Payout for a "single bet" is equal to the sum of the bet multiplied by the winning coefficient.
    2. «Express bet» – bet on the outcome of several events that do not depend on each other. The payment at the Express bet is equal to the product of the coefficients of each event multiplied by the bet size. In the "Express" can be included several unrelated events, any kind of sport. The express bet is counted (it is considered a winning one) if all the selected events were correctly anticipated. In the event that at least one event was not predicted correctly, the bet is considered to be a losing one.
    3. «System» – full combination of "Expresses" of a single size, chosen by the bettor. Payout for "System" is equal to the amount of payments for "Express", included in the "System".
    4. «Live – bet» - a bet on the main or additional outcome made during the event.
    5. «Handicap» - providing a virtual advantage, or vice versa easing. That is, if the outcome of the match is predictable, since one of the teams has a clear advantage, the user can bet the weaker team, thereby adding to it the number of virtual goals that he chooses. If the handicap is + 1 - one goal, H + 2 - 2 goals and so on. Thus, the match for the user will start if the user puts on the handicap+3 for a certain team, the game will start with a score of 3: 0 in favor of the selected team. Such a bet is considered to be winning if the selected team won regarding a handicap. In case of a tie, the bet is considered to be a losing bet.
    6. «Total» - is the bet on the total number of goals scored, points scored, played games in the match. The total can have two positions - more or less than the selected number. The bet will be returned if the number coincides with the total.
    7. «Bet on time » - a betting, which consists in the fact that the event will occur at the time specified by the user. For example, the time of the first goal, the first corner, and so on. Time for payments at these rates will be determined without taking into account the seconds. The Company has the right to set the maximum or minimum amount of the bet on any event or any type of bet. The victory of the first participant of the event is designated as W1, the tie is designated as X, the victory of the second participant is marked as W2.
